    Presents an intriguing alternative to traditional chess, offering a familiar yet distinct approach to the game. With traditional chess still as base with a peculiar twiste, its Normal chess, but abnormal. This project is a 1v1 turn-based online competitive game design for a battle of witts.
Rules

  1. You can only move once a turn.
  2. After moving it ends the turn.
  3. You can only promote once a turn.
  4. After Promoting you cant move the piece that was promoted.
  5. To promote you need to be in the right zone in the board.

    6. Board promotion area, Both can promote in the green area.


Game Instructions
  • To move you need to click on the piece you want to move then the space you moving to.
  • You gain shards by moving your pieces.
  • To promote you first need a card made by two shards after that click on the card you want, then on the pawn you promoting.
  • To unlock the next promoten type you need to lose or capture a piece.
  • To win you just need to capture the enemey's king.
